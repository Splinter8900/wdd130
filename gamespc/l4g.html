Looking 4 Group

If you want to be good at competitve gaming and have an edge on the competition, then you need a team. Every competitve game requires good communication and teamwork for you to be succesful, and most of the time, queueing with random players just doesnt cut it. Below are some resources to help you find your dream team!

Discord

Discord is a tool where users can create and join servers based around all sorts of different games and communities. On these servers users can interact with each other via text based chat rooms or voice and video chat rooms. Users can share content with each other and can show each other what games they are currently playing and interested in. There are many servers that have chat rooms based around looking for groups to play with. To use discord you just need to make a free account and search for the servers that you may be interested in a join.

Discord Servers

Reddit

Reddit is a huge collection of online forums. Users can create sub-reddits around any topic, which allows people to congregate and share their own insights and ideas about said topics. Reddit can be used to get help or to help someone else out with what every they need including gaming. It is almost a guerantee that every game out there has a sub-reddit and if there isnt one, you can be the one to make it.

Reddit

Twitch

Twitch is a streaming platform primarily used by the gaming community. Streamers can stream whatever they are playing or doing onto the site for millions to watch and interact through the chat. The reason Twitch is included here is because you can find people with similar gaming interests while you watch people play the games that you are interested in. Not only does this help you find people to play with but lets you learn the games by watching others play.
