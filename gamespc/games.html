Overwatch 2

Overwatch 2 is a first-person, team-based, hero shooter where you compete in teams of 6 in various game modes. There is a big variety of heros you can play as, each with their own unique skills and abilities to help you and your team acheive victory.

Overwatch Wiki

Gundam Evolution

Gundam Evolution is a first person, team based shooter, where you play as different mobile suits from the Gundam universe. Each mobile suit is well balanced with their own set of abilities and movement. There are no class roles which sets this game apart from games like overwatch for example.

Gundam Evolution Wiki

Rainbow Six: Siege

Rainbow Six: Siege is a tactical first-person shooter where you play as counter-terrorism operators each with their own unique kit and ability. The game play loop faces you against another team of 5 in an attack/ defend scenario. The attackers have to infiltrate the building to complete an objective that the defenders protect with various traps, gadgets and reinforcements. What sets siege apart is the destructive environments allowing you to create new routes and lines of sight by destrying walls and floors.

R6S Wiki

Call of Duty: Warzone 2.0

Call of Duty: Warzone 2.0 falls into the battle royale category of games. You and your team, as well as 150 other players, drop from the sky into] a large map where you collect weapons and equipment to help you survive. Completeing contracts allows you to earn money to buy better gear and equipment while a deadly circle of gas closes in making the play space smaller and smaller. The goal is to be the last squad or player standing as you fight other players over increasingly smaller play space.

WZ 2.0 Wiki

Counter Strike: Global Offensive

Counter Strike: Global Offensive, or csgo for short, is a tactical first person shooter. You and your team fight to either plant a bomb, or prevent a bomb from being planted against another team of players. Your loadout is available to purchase in the form of guns, grenade and other equipment from a buy menu at the start of the round. Each round ends when one team is eliminated or the objective is complete in a best of 16 rounds. The maps generally have 2 options of bombsite to attack or defend for each team.

CS:GO Wiki

Valorant

Valorant is a unique shooter that combines tactical bomb defusal gameplay, with hero shooter mechanics. You play as various agents in teams of 5, each with their own unique abilities. This is suplemented by a buy phase at the beggining of each round for your weapons and equipment.

Valorant Wiki

League of Legends

League of Legends is a MOBA, which stands for Multiplayer Online Battle Arena. Like many competitive games L.o.L. is based around a champion system with a current total of 162 champions, each with their own unique role and abilities. League is played from a top down perspective as each team fights through ai minions and players to destroy a sequence of turrents to ultimately destroy the enemy nexus. This is accomplished by spending gold earned throughout the match to buy items that buff and augment your character and their abilities.

LoL Wiki

Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a hero based, battle royale shooter, set in the Titanfall universe. The same premise of a map full of loot and weapons and 100 players all trying to be the last ones standing in an ever shrinking map is present. But what makes apex legends unique is the legends that you play as. Each one has unique abilities that allow you to try different playstyles and gives a sort of rock paper scissors dynamic against your foes. This result in fun cat and mouse gameplay as you use your tools to the best of your ability to outsmart your enemies.

Apex Legends Wiki

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is a 2-D platformer fighting game on the Nintendo Switch, published by Nintendo. It includes all the characters from the previous installments, that you play as on various stages, fighting other players to knock them off the stage. Each character has a unique set of abilities and combos to try. This games requires high reflexes and skill and has a very high skill ceiling while still being fun for the casual player as well..

Smash Bros. Ultimate Wiki

Fortnite

Fortnite is a third-person, battle royale shooter. What makes Fortnite unique is its building mechanic which allows players to put up their own cover and protection anywhere in the map. Players harvest materials to be able to build said structures to their advantage to overcome the enemy. Fortnite also has many in game challenges and narrative events \ that build and flush out the lore of the in-game universe.

Fortnite Wiki
